Common Funding Application

This is your one-stop source to apply for event funding from a variety of sources accross the university.

For student groups, you simply create an event, fill in the required information, and we recommend what funding sources to apply to. They then indicate what funding you will receive, and you get notifications along the way. Register now to get started!

For funding sources, you see all the events of every student group that has applied to you in one view. You can see how much other funding sources have granted and for what items and keep groups accountable.

Questions or feedback? Use the "Feedback" button on the screen. If you're a funding source and want to get on the Common Funding App, e-mail Nikolai Zapertov (nikolaiz@sas). us at commonfundingapp@pennapps.com
